# Kldekari (mountain)

Kldekari (Georgian: კლდეკარი) is a mountain, with an elevation of 2,000 meters above sea level, and a rocky pass of the Trialeti range in the Shida Kartli and Kvemo Kartli region of southeastern Georgia. In the vicinity are the ruins of the medieval Kldekari Fortress.
