# Pir Sabz, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad

Pir Sabz (Persian: پيرسبز, also Romanized as Pīr Sabz) is a village in Emamzadeh Jafar Rural District, in the Central District of Gachsaran County, Kohgiluyeh and Boyer-Ahmad Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 24, in 7 families.
