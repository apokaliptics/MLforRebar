# Ilinčić

Ilinčić is a Serbian surname (Serbian Cyrillic: Илинчић). Notable people with the surname include:

Roksanda Ilinčić (born 1975), Serbian fashion designer
Zlatko Ilinčić (born 1968), Serbian chess grandmaster
