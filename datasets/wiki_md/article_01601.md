# Vladikavkaz railway station

Vladikavkaz (Russian: Владикавказ, Ossetian: Дзæуджыхъæу) is a railway station in Vladikavkaz, the capital of North Ossetia in Russia. The terminus station on the Beslan – Vladikavkaz line (Vladikavkaz Railway).
