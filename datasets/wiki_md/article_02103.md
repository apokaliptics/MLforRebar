# Oscar Bustos Aburto

Oscar Bustos Aburto (18 March 1887 – 1 August 1974) was a Chilean teacher, psychologist, and Radical Party politician who served as Deputy for the 22nd Departamental Group (Valdivia, La Unión and Río Bueno) during the 1949–1953 legislative period. He also served as Minister of Education during the government of President Juan Antonio Ríos.
