# Kaničky

Kaničky is a municipality and village in Domažlice District in the Plzeň Region of the Czech Republic. It has about 30 inhabitants.
Kaničky lies approximately 17 kilometres (11 mi) east of Domažlice, 35 km (22 mi) south-west of Plzeň, and 114 km (71 mi) south-west of Prague.
