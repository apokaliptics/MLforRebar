# Grabina, Łódź East County

Grabina [ɡraˈbina] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Nowosolna, within Łódź East County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 11 km (7 mi) north-east of the regional capital Łódź.
