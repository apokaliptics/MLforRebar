# Katrín Lea Elenudóttir

Katrín Lea Elenudóttir (born Ekaterina Skorobogatova, Russian: Екатерина Скоробогатова; 22 February 1999) is an Icelandic model and beauty pageant titleholder who was crowned Miss Universe Iceland 2018. She represented Iceland at Miss Universe 2018 pageant in Bangkok, Thailand.
