# 2013 Butler Bulldogs women's soccer team

The 2013 Butler Bulldogs women's soccer team represented Butler University in the 2013 NCAA Division I women's soccer season. Their head coaches were Tari St. John, serving her 7th year, and Rob Alman, serving his 1st year. The Bulldogs played their home games at the Butler Bowl.
