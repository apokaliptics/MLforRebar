# 1989 Greek Super Cup

The 1989 Greek Super Cup was the 4th edition of the Greek Super Cup, an association football match contested by the winners of the previous season's Alpha Ethniki and Greek Cup competitions. The match took place on 30 August 1989 at the Athens Olympic Stadium. The contesting teams were the 1988–89 Alpha Ethniki champions, AEK Athens and the 1988–89 Greek Cup winners, Panathinaikos.
