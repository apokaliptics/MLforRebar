# Haji Khan

Haji Khan or Haji Abdulaziz Khan (Persian: حاجی عبدالعزیز خان) was the fourth khan of Shaki. He was described as a brave and courageous, but extremely ruthless man by Azerbaijani historian Abbasgulu Bakikhanov.
