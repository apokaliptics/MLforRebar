# Maria Tsiartsiani

Maria Tsiartsiani (Greek: Μαρία Τσιαρτσανη; born 21 October 1980 in Thessaloniki) is a Greek Olympic beach volleyballer.   She competed at the 2008 Summer Olympics, partnering with Efthalia Koutroumanidou.  At the 2012 Summer Olympics, she competed with Vassiliki Arvaniti.
