# Guyim, Shiraz

Guyim (Persian: گويم, also Romanized as Gūyīm; also known as Gūyom and Gūyum) is a town in Derak Rural District, in the Central District of Shiraz County, Fars province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 7,297, in 1,829 families.
