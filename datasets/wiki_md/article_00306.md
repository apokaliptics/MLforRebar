# Molla Ali, Sistan and Baluchestan

Molla Ali (Persian: ملاعلي, also Romanized as Mollā ‘Alī) is a village in Qorqori Rural District, Qorqori District, Hirmand County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 897, in 181 families.
