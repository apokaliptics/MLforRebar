# Peter Hoagland

Peter Jackson Hoagland (November 17, 1941 â€“ October 30, 2007) was an American politician from the U.S. state of Nebraska. A member of the Democratic Party, Hoagland represented Nebraska's 2nd congressional district in the U.S. House of Representatives from 1989 to 1995. To date, he is the last Democrat to represent Nebraska in the House of Representatives for more than one term.
