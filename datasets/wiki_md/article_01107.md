# Airjet Exploração Aérea de Carga

Airjet Exploração Aérea de Carga (a.k.a. Airjet) is an airline based in Angola. It was established in 2003 and operates 3  aircraft. Airjet joined the JetVision Holdings (South Africa) on 1 May 2019 as an airline partner. Airjet's head offices are located in Luanda, Angola and Pretoria, South Africa. It is banned in EU airspace like most other Angolan airlines.
