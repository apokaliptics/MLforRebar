# Gojców

Gojców [ˈɡɔi̯t͡suf] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Opatów, within Opatów County, Świętokrzyskie Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 6 km (4 mi) south of Opatów and 58 km (36 mi) east of the regional capital Kielce.
