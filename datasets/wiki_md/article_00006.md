# Bad End Theater

Bad End Theater (stylized in all caps) is a 2021 visual novel video game developed by indie developer NomnomNami and published by NomnomNami and Serenity Forge. Set in the titular theater, the player controls the actions of the main cast to explore different bad endings from the perspectives of each character. It was released for Windows on 26 October 2021 via Steam. It was released for consoles on 10 July 2025. Bad End Theater has received generally positive reviews, with reviewers praising the characters, gameplay, and story.
