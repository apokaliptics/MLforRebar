# DXGS

DXGS (765 AM) Radyo Pilipino is a radio station owned and operated by Radyo Pilipino Corporation through its licensee Radio Audience Developers Integrated Organization (RADIO), Inc. The station's studio is located in Brgy. Lagao, General Santos.  The station broadcasts daily from 5:00 AM to 7:00 PM.
Established in 1965, DXGS is the pioneer station in the city. It was formerly owned by Filipinas Broadcasting Network until 1983, when it was among the stations sold to RadioCorp.
