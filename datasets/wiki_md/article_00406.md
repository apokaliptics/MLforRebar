# Agvali

Agvali (Russian: Агвали, Avar: Агъвали) is a rural locality (a selo) and the administrative center of Tsumadinsky District of the Republic of Dagestan, Russia. Population:  2,455 (2010 census); 2,016 (2002 census); 2,101 (1989 Soviet census).
