# Botswana Cricket Association

The Botswana Cricket Association (BCA) is the official governing body of the sport of cricket in Botswana. Its headquarters is in the national capital Gaborone, at the Botswana Cricket Association Oval. It is affiliated with Botswana National Sports Council  (BNSC) and the Botswana National Olympic Committee (BNOC). Established in 1983, the BCA has been a member of the International Cricket Council (ICC) since 2002, and was a founding member of the Africa Cricket Association.
