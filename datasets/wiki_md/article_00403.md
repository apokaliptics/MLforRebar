# Posht-e Shek

Posht-e Shek (Persian: پشت شك) is a village in Chah Dadkhoda Rural District, Chah Dadkhoda District, Qaleh Ganj County, Kerman Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 149, in 25 families.
