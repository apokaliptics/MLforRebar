# Nazara Technologies

Nazara Technologies is an Indian multinational technology company that has business interests in mobile games, esports, and sports media. Founded in 1999, it is based in Mumbai, India, and through the years acquired several companies turned into subsidiaries, including WildWorks, Curve Games, and Sportskeeda.
