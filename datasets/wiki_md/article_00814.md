# Vladimir Mirzoyev

Vladimir Vladimirovich Mirzoyev (Russian: Влади́мир Влади́мирович Мирзо́ев; born October 21, 1957) is a Soviet and Russian film director, set designer and teacher. Winner of State Prize of the Russian Federation.
