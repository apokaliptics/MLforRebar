# Dimapur–Kohima Highway

The Dimapur–Kohima Highway is a tolled four lane dual carriageway in Nagaland that connects the state's largest city Dimapur and second largest city Kohima. Scheduled to be opened in 2021, the 42 km (26.1 mi) highway will reduce the travel time between Dimapur and Kohima from 2.5 hours to 1 hour.
