# Kedah Royal Mausoleum

Kedah Royal Mausoleum or Langgar Royal Mausoleum (Malay: Makam Diraja Langgar) is a royal burial ground in the Kedah state of Malaysia. It is located in Langgar, in Kota Setar District.
