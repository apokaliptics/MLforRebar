# A. T. Ramaswamy

A. T. Ramaswamy is a former Member of the Legislative Assembly in Karnataka. He has been elected to the Karnataka Legislative Assembly four times from the Arkalgud Vidhana Sabha constituency (1989, 1994, 2004, 2018).
The environmental organisation “Parisarakkagi Naavu,” led by former MLA and environmentalist A.T. Ramaswamy, will officially be launched on April 12. The initiative aims to unite environmental experts, students, citizens, and organisations across the state to work together for environmental conservation.
