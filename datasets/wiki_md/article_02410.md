# Kietlin, Łódź Voivodeship

Kietlin [ˈkʲɛtlin] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Radomsko, within Radomsko County, Łódź Voivodeship, in south-central Poland. It lies approximately 5 km (3 mi) north-east of Radomsko and 78 km (48 mi) south of the regional capital Łódź.
