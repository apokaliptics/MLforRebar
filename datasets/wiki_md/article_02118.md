# Taraska

Taraska [taˈraska] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Aleksandrów, within Piotrków County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately 8 km (5 mi) north-west of Aleksandrów, 19 km (12 mi) south-east of Piotrków Trybunalski, and 61 km (38 mi) south-east of the regional capital Łódź.
