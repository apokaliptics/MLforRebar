# Que Mundo é Esse?

Que Mundo é Esse?  (transl. What kind of world is that?) is a Brazilian travel documentary television series that premiered on GloboNews on July 26, 2015. The series features André Fran, Felipe UFO, Michel Coeli and  Rodrigo Cebrian who explores various countries, cultures and their social, political and economic issues.
