# Franciszkowo, Świecie County

Franciszkowo [frant͡ɕiʂˈkɔvɔ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Bukowiec, within Świecie County, Kuyavian-Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-central Poland.
