# Avezac-Prat-Lahitte

Avezac-Prat-Lahitte (French pronunciation: [avzak pʁat la.it] ; Occitan: Avesac, Prat e era Hita) is a commune in the Hautes-Pyrénées department in southwestern France. It was created in 1973 by the merger of two former communes: Lahitte and Avezac-Prat.
