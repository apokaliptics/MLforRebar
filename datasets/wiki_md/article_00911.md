# C'est pour toi (song)

"C'est pour toi" (meaning "It's For You") is the first single from Celine Dion's album C'est pour toi. It was released in August 1985 in Quebec, Canada. On 21 September 1985 the song entered the Quebec Singles Chart and reached number three, spending 20 weeks on the chart. It included as B-side another album track called "Pour vous". A music video was made for the C'est pour toi TV special in 1985. "C'est pour toi" was featured also on Dion's later compilations: Les chansons en or (1986) and The Best Of (1988).
