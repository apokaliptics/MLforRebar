# Cabañas Raras

Cabañas Raras (Spanish pronunciation: [kaˈβaɲas ˈraɾas]) is a village and municipality located in the region of El Bierzo (province of León, Castile and León, Spain) . According to the 2010 census (INE), the municipality has a population of 1,329 inhabitants.
