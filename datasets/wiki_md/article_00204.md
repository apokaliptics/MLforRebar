# Finny the Fish & the Seven Waters

Finny the Fish & the Seven Waters, known in Japan as Uo: Seven Waters and the Gods of Legend, is an action-adventure video game developed by Sega Wow and published by Sony Computer Entertainment for the PlayStation 2. It was released in Japan in 2004 and by Natsume Inc. in North America in 2005. It was a collaborative effort between Sega and Sony Computer Entertainment.
