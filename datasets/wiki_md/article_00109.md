# Abdulla Nazim Ibrahim

Abdulla Nazim Ibrahim (Dhivehi: ޢަބުދުﷲ ނާޡިމް އިބްރާހީމް) is a Maldivian politician who is currently serving as the Minister of Health. He served the same position from 2016 to 2018. He also served as a Deputy Minister of Education.
