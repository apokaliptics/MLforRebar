Borough in Western Norway, Norway

**Trones og Sentrum** is a [borough](/wiki/Borough "Borough") of the [city of Sandnes](/wiki/Sandnes_(town) "Sandnes (town)") in the west part of the large municipality of [Sandnes](/wiki/Sandnes "Sandnes") in [Rogaland](/wiki/Rogaland "Rogaland") county, [Norway](/wiki/Norway "Norway"). It encompasses the downtown part of the city, at the inner end of the [Gandsfjorden](/wiki/Gandsfjorden "Gandsfjorden"). In 2016, this borough had a population of 8,250[[1]](#cite_note-SK-1) and is 2.5 km2 (0.97 sq mi) in size. [Sandnes Church](/wiki/Sandnes_Church_(Rogaland) "Sandnes Church (Rogaland)") is located in this borough.
