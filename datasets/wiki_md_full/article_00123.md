Species of fly

***Anthrax varius*** is a species of [bee fly](/wiki/Bee_fly "Bee fly") in the family [Bombyliidae](/wiki/Bombyliidae "Bombyliidae"), first described by [Johan Christian Fabricius](/wiki/Johan_Christian_Fabricius "Johan Christian Fabricius") in 1794.[[1]](#cite_note-1) It is found across much of [Europe](/wiki/Europe "Europe"), and parts of northern [Africa](/wiki/Africa "Africa") and the [Middle East](/wiki/Middle_East "Middle East").[[2]](#cite_note-worldcat-2)
