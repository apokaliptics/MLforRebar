Baseball player

**Bailey Landry** (born September 25, 1995) was an American [softball](/wiki/Softball "Softball") player for the [Scrap Yard Fast Pitch](/wiki/Scrap_Yard_Fast_Pitch "Scrap Yard Fast Pitch"). She attended [East Ascension High School](/wiki/East_Ascension_High_School "East Ascension High School") in [Gonzales, Louisiana](/wiki/Gonzales,_Louisiana "Gonzales, Louisiana").[[1]](#cite_note-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2)[[3]](#cite_note-3) She later attended [Louisiana State University](/wiki/Louisiana_State_University "Louisiana State University"), where she was a two-time [All-American](/wiki/All-American "All-American") for the [LSU Tigers softball](/wiki/LSU_Tigers_softball "LSU Tigers softball") team.[[4]](#cite_note-4)[[5]](#cite_note-5)[[6]](#cite_note-6) Landry led the Tigers to three [Women's College World Series](/wiki/Women%27s_College_World_Series "Women's College World Series") appearances in [2015](/wiki/2015_Women%27s_College_World_Series "2015 Women's College World Series"), [2016](/wiki/2016_Women%27s_College_World_Series "2016 Women's College World Series") and [2017](/wiki/2017_Women%27s_College_World_Series "2017 Women's College World Series"). She later went on to play professional softball with the [Texas Charge](/wiki/Texas_Charge "Texas Charge") of [National Pro Fastpitch](/wiki/National_Pro_Fastpitch "National Pro Fastpitch").[[7]](#cite_note-7)[[8]](#cite_note-8)
