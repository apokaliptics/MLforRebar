Village in Lubusz Voivodeship, Poland

**Gaj** [[ɡai̯]](/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish "Help:IPA/Polish") is a [village](/wiki/Village "Village") in the administrative district of [Gmina Przytoczna](/wiki/Gmina_Przytoczna "Gmina Przytoczna"), within [Międzyrzecz County](/wiki/Mi%C4%99dzyrzecz_County "Międzyrzecz County"), [Lubusz Voivodeship](/wiki/Lubusz_Voivodeship "Lubusz Voivodeship"), in western Poland.[[1]](#cite_note-TERYT-1)
