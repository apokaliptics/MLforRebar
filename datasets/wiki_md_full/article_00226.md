Andorran judoka

**Laura Sallés López** (born 15 February 1986) is a practitioner of [judo](/wiki/Judo "Judo") from Andorra. She has participated in several World Championships and has won several medals at the Games of Small States. Sallés participated in the [2016 Summer Olympics](/wiki/2016_Summer_Olympics "2016 Summer Olympics"), where she was the flag bearer for her country.[[1]](#cite_note-1) In the [Women's 63 kg](/wiki/Judo_at_the_2016_Summer_Olympics_%E2%80%93_Women%27s_63_kg "Judo at the 2016 Summer Olympics – Women's 63 kg"), she lost to [Katharina Haecker](/wiki/Katharina_Haecker_(judoka) "Katharina Haecker (judoka)") in the first round.
