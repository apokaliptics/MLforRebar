Genus of wasps

***Astichomyiia*** is a genus of [hymenopteran](/wiki/Hymenoptera "Hymenoptera") [insects](/wiki/Insect "Insect") of the family [Eulophidae](/wiki/Eulophidae "Eulophidae").
