Genus of plants

***Kundmannia*** is a genus of [flowering plants](/wiki/Flowering_plant "Flowering plant") belonging to the family [Apiaceae](/wiki/Apiaceae "Apiaceae").[[1]](#cite_note-POWO-1)

Its native range is the Mediterranean. It is found in Algeria, the [Balearic Islands](/wiki/Balearic_Islands "Balearic Islands"), Corsica, Greece, Italy, Crete, Morocco, Portugal, Sardinia, Sicily, Spain, Tunisia and Turkey.[[1]](#cite_note-POWO-1)

The genus name of *Kundmannia* is in honour of Johann Christian Kundmann (1684–1751), a German doctor from [Wrocław](/wiki/Wroc%C5%82aw "Wrocław") with a large naturalist collection.[[2]](#cite_note-2)
It was first described and published in Intr. Hist. Nat. on page 116 in 1777.[[1]](#cite_note-POWO-1)

Known species, according to Kew:[[1]](#cite_note-POWO-1)

* *[Kundmannia anatolica](/w/index.php?title=Kundmannia_anatolica&action=edit&redlink=1 "Kundmannia anatolica (page does not exist)")* Hub.-Mor.
* *[Kundmannia sicula](/wiki/Kundmannia_sicula "Kundmannia sicula")* (L.) DC.
* *[Kundmannia syriaca](/w/index.php?title=Kundmannia_syriaca&action=edit&redlink=1 "Kundmannia syriaca (page does not exist)")* Boiss.
