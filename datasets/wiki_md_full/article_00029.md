International sporting event

The **women's 200 metre backstroke** competition of the [swimming](/wiki/Swimming_(sport) "Swimming (sport)") events at the **[1967 Pan American Games](/wiki/1967_Pan_American_Games "1967 Pan American Games")** took place on 26 July at the [Pan Am Pool](/wiki/Pan_Am_Pool "Pan Am Pool").[[1]](#cite_note-div-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2) It was the first appearance of this event in the Pan American Games.[[3]](#cite_note-3)[[4]](#cite_note-4)[[5]](#cite_note-5)[[6]](#cite_note-6)[[7]](#cite_note-7)[[8]](#cite_note-8)

This race consisted of four lengths of the pool, all in [backstroke](/wiki/Backstroke "Backstroke").[[9]](#cite_note-manual-9)
