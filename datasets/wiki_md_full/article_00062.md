2011 film

***The City of Children*** ([Greek](/wiki/Greek_language "Greek language"): Η Πόλη των Παιδιών, [translit.](/wiki/Transliteration "Transliteration") I poli ton paidion) is a 2011 Greek [drama film](/wiki/Drama_film "Drama film") directed by [Giorgos Gikapeppas](/w/index.php?title=Giorgos_Gikapeppas&action=edit&redlink=1 "Giorgos Gikapeppas (page does not exist)").[[1]](#cite_note-1)[[2]](#cite_note-chinokino-2)
