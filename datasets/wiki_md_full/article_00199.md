1995 studio album by Gene

***Olympian*** is the 1995 debut album by British [rock](/wiki/Rock_music "Rock music") band [Gene](/wiki/Gene_(band) "Gene (band)"), released on 20 March 1995 by Costermonger Records.

It is frequently recognized as one of the crowning achievements of [Britpop](/wiki/Britpop "Britpop").[[1]](#cite_note-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2)[[3]](#cite_note-3)

The album sound is noted for being influenced by [the Smiths](/wiki/The_Smiths "The Smiths"), [the Jam](/wiki/The_Jam "The Jam") and [the Small Faces](/wiki/The_Small_Faces "The Small Faces").[[4]](#cite_note-4)[[5]](#cite_note-5)

The album reached number 8 in the UK albums chart].[[6]](#cite_note-6)

The image used for the cover is a still from [Ingmar Bergman](/wiki/Ingmar_Bergman "Ingmar Bergman")'s *[The Seventh Seal](/wiki/The_Seventh_Seal "The Seventh Seal")*.

## Reception

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Olympian_(album)&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Reception")]

[Stephen Thomas Erlewine](/wiki/Stephen_Thomas_Erlewine "Stephen Thomas Erlewine") of [AllMusic](/wiki/AllMusic "AllMusic") said of the album: "While Gene manages to carve out an identity indebted to the Smiths but not dominated by them, they also fail to produce an album of consistently compelling material – considering that it's a debut album, that's not a fatal flaw. And Gene's best material shows they are capable of transcending their influences."[[7]](#cite_note-allmusic-7)
