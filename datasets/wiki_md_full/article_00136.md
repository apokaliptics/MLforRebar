1978 studio album by Prince Far I

***Long Life*** is a [reggae](/wiki/Reggae "Reggae") album by [Prince Far I](/wiki/Prince_Far_I "Prince Far I"), released in 1978 through [Front Line](/wiki/Front_Line_(record_label) "Front Line (record label)").[[1]](#cite_note-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2) "Black Starliner Must Come" is about [Marcus Garvey](/wiki/Marcus_Garvey "Marcus Garvey")'s [Black Star Line](/wiki/Black_Star_Line "Black Star Line").[[3]](#cite_note-3)

## Critical reception

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Long_Life&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Critical reception")]

[AllMusic](/wiki/AllMusic "AllMusic") wrote that "the rhythms are provided courtesy of a studio band made up of members of both Soul Syndicate (notably the killer bass-and-drums duo of Robbie Shakespeare and Sly Dunbar) and Roots Radics, and they are, without exception, as solid and heavy as a bag of boulders."[[4]](#cite_note-AM-4)
