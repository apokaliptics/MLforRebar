**Eugene Russell Smith** (November 11, 1895 - June 13, 1948) was a professional [football](/wiki/American_football "American football") player during the early years of the [National Football League](/wiki/National_Football_League "National Football League") (NFL) with the [Chicago Staleys/Bears](/wiki/Chicago_Bears "Chicago Bears"), [Canton Bulldogs](/wiki/Canton_Bulldogs "Canton Bulldogs"), [Milwaukee Badgers](/wiki/Milwaukee_Badgers "Milwaukee Badgers"), [Cleveland Bulldogs](/wiki/Cleveland_Bulldogs "Cleveland Bulldogs"), [Detroit Panthers](/wiki/Detroit_Panthers_(NFL) "Detroit Panthers (NFL)") and [Hammond Pros](/wiki/Hammond_Pros "Hammond Pros"). Smith won NFL championships with the Staleys in [1921](/wiki/1921_NFL_season "1921 NFL season"), the Canton Bulldogs in [1923](/wiki/1923_NFL_season "1923 NFL season") and the Cleveland Bulldogs in [1924](/wiki/1924_NFL_season "1924 NFL season"). He played a total of 50 games in the NFL.[[2]](#cite_note-2)
