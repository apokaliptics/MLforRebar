1990 Post apocalyptic tabletop role-playing game supplement

***Survivors' Guide to the United Kingdom*** is a supplement published by [Game Designers' Workshop](/wiki/Game_Designers%27_Workshop "Game Designers' Workshop") (GDW) in 1990 for the post-apocalyptic role-playing game *[Twilight: 2000](/wiki/Twilight:_2000 "Twilight: 2000")*.

## Content

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Survivors%27_Guide_to_the_United_Kingdom&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Content")]

In the original *Twilight: 2000* game, many of the first adventures centred around a military unit stranded in Central Europe following a fictional World War III known in the game as the "Twilight War". *Survivors' Guide to the United Kingdom* gives details of how to create a campaign setting for adventures set in Great Britain.[[1]](#cite_note-HW-1)

The book covers:

* Year by year effects of World War III on Great Britain, 1996–2000
* Subsequent changes to geography, living conditions, the economy, and availabler armaments
* A guide to various regions
* Notable personalities
* Various military and militia units and intelligence agencies
* Random encounters

## Publication history

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Survivors%27_Guide_to_the_United_Kingdom&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Publication history")]

GDW published the first edition of *Twilight: 2000* in 1984. Many adventures and supplements followed including *Survivors' Guide to the United Kingdom* in 1990, a 48-page softcover book written by [Peter Phillipps](/w/index.php?title=Peter_Phillipps_(game_designer)&action=edit&redlink=1 "Peter Phillipps (game designer) (page does not exist)"), with interior art by [Kirk Wescom](/w/index.php?title=Kirk_Wescom&action=edit&redlink=1 "Kirk Wescom (page does not exist)") and [Tim Bradstreet](/wiki/Tim_Bradstreet "Tim Bradstreet"), and cover art by Radley Masinelli.[[1]](#cite_note-HW-1)

*Survivors' Guide to the United Kingdom* was one of the last supplements published for the first edition of *Twilight: 2000*, since shortly after its release, GDW published a second edition of the game.

## Reception

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Survivors%27_Guide_to_the_United_Kingdom&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Reception")]

Allen Mixson reviewed the product in a 1991 issue of *[White Wolf Magazine](/wiki/White_Wolf_Magazine "White Wolf Magazine")*, highly recommending it and rating it a 4 out of a possible 5 overall.[[2]](#cite_note-Mixson91-2)

In a review of *Survivors' Guide to the United Kingdom* in *[Black Gate](/wiki/Black_Gate_(magazine) "Black Gate (magazine)")*, Patrick Kanouse said "The *Survivor's Guide to the United Kingdom* is the only official supplement by the late GDW that covers the isles. And the situation is not good in the old country."[[3]](#cite_note-3)
