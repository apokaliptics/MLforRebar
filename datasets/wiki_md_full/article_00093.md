British historical drama film

***Pressure*** is an upcoming British [war film](/wiki/War_film "War film") directed by [Anthony Maras](/wiki/Anthony_Maras "Anthony Maras") and written by Maras and [David Haig](/wiki/David_Haig "David Haig"), based on Haig's [2014 stage play](/wiki/Pressure_(play) "Pressure (play)"). It stars [Andrew Scott](/wiki/Andrew_Scott_(actor) "Andrew Scott (actor)") as meteorologist [James Stagg](/wiki/James_Stagg "James Stagg") and [Brendan Fraser](/wiki/Brendan_Fraser "Brendan Fraser") as [Dwight D. Eisenhower](/wiki/Dwight_D._Eisenhower "Dwight D. Eisenhower").

## Premise

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Pressure_(upcoming_film)&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Premise")]

In the final days before the planned Allied invasion of Europe in 1944, [Royal Air Force](/wiki/Royal_Air_Force "Royal Air Force") meteorologist [James Stagg](/wiki/James_Stagg "James Stagg") must deliver a weather report to the Allied high command determining the feasibility of the planned [D-Day](/wiki/D-Day "D-Day").[[1]](#cite_note-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2)

## Production

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Pressure_(upcoming_film)&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Production")]

The film is produced by [Working Title Films](/wiki/Working_Title_Films "Working Title Films") and [StudioCanal](/wiki/StudioCanal "StudioCanal") and directed by Anthony Maras, who co-wrote the script with [David Haig](/wiki/David_Haig "David Haig"). It is adapted from Haig's stage play *Pressure* which premiered in Edinburgh in 2014 and later moved to the West End.[[3]](#cite_note-3)

[Andrew Scott](/wiki/Andrew_Scott_(actor) "Andrew Scott (actor)") was reported to be leading the cast in July 2024 as Captain [James Stagg](/wiki/James_Stagg "James Stagg").[[4]](#cite_note-4) The same month, [Brendan Fraser](/wiki/Brendan_Fraser "Brendan Fraser") was cast as [Dwight D. Eisenhower](/wiki/Dwight_D._Eisenhower "Dwight D. Eisenhower").[[5]](#cite_note-5) The following month, [Kerry Condon](/wiki/Kerry_Condon "Kerry Condon") joined the cast as Eisenhower's assistant [Captain Kay Summersby](/wiki/Kay_Summersby "Kay Summersby").[[6]](#cite_note-6) They were later joined in the cast by [Damien Lewis](/wiki/Damian_Lewis "Damian Lewis") and [Chris Messina](/wiki/Chris_Messina "Chris Messina"). [Principal photography](/wiki/Principal_photography "Principal photography") began in the UK in September 2024.[[7]](#cite_note-7)[[8]](#cite_note-8)

## Release

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Pressure_(upcoming_film)&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Release")]

In November 2024, [Focus Features](/wiki/Focus_Features "Focus Features") acquired U.S. distribution rights to the film.[[9]](#cite_note-9)
