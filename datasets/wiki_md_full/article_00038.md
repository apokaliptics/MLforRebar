Species of moth

***Ancylolomia lentifascialis*** is a [moth](/wiki/Moth "Moth") in the family [Crambidae](/wiki/Crambidae "Crambidae"). It was described by [George Hampson](/wiki/George_Hampson "George Hampson") in 1919.[[1]](#cite_note-1) It is found in [South Africa](/wiki/South_Africa "South Africa"), where it has been recorded from the province of [Gauteng](/wiki/Gauteng "Gauteng").[[2]](#cite_note-2)
