Indian actress (born 2001)

**Saiee Manjrekar** (born 24 December 2001) is an Indian actress who works in [Telugu](/wiki/Telugu_cinema "Telugu cinema") and [Hindi](/wiki/Hindi_cinema "Hindi cinema") films. The daughter of actors [Mahesh Manjrekar](/wiki/Mahesh_Manjrekar "Mahesh Manjrekar") and [Medha Manjrekar](/wiki/Medha_Manjrekar "Medha Manjrekar"), she made her acting debut with *[Dabangg 3](/wiki/Dabangg_3 "Dabangg 3")* (2019). Manjrekar has since appeared in the Telugu films *[Ghani](/wiki/Ghani_(2022_film) "Ghani (2022 film)")* (2022), *[Major](/wiki/Major_(film) "Major (film)")* (2022) and *[Skanda](/wiki/Skanda_(film) "Skanda (film)")* (2023).[[1]](#cite_note-1)

## Early life

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Saiee_Manjrekar&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Early life")]

Manjrekar was born 24 December 2001 to film actors [Medha](/wiki/Medha_Manjrekar "Medha Manjrekar") and [Mahesh Manjrekar](/wiki/Mahesh_Manjrekar "Mahesh Manjrekar").[[2]](#cite_note-2)[[3]](#cite_note-3)[[4]](#cite_note-4)

## Career

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Saiee_Manjrekar&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Career")]

Manjrekar made her film debut with a brief appearance as Kushi Damle in the Marathi film *[Kaksparsh](/wiki/Kaksparsh "Kaksparsh")* (2012). She did her first leading role as Khushi Chautala in the 2019 [Hindi](/wiki/Hindi_cinema "Hindi cinema") action-comedy *[Dabangg 3](/wiki/Dabangg_3 "Dabangg 3")* opposite [Salman Khan](/wiki/Salman_Khan "Salman Khan").[[5]](#cite_note-5) The film received negative reviews from critics, and its commercial prospects were affected by the [CAA protests](/wiki/CAA_protests "CAA protests"). It earned her a nomination for [Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut](/wiki/Filmfare_Award_for_Best_Female_Debut "Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut"). In 2020, she appeared in the music video for the song "Manjha", alongside [Aayush Sharma](/wiki/Aayush_Sharma "Aayush Sharma").[[6]](#cite_note-mv:indtod-6)

In 2022, Manjrekar made her [Telugu film](/wiki/Telugu_cinema "Telugu cinema") debut alongside [Varun Tej](/wiki/Varun_Tej "Varun Tej") in *[Ghani](/wiki/Ghani_(2022_film) "Ghani (2022 film)")*, which ended up being a commercial failure. She was next featured in the Telugu-Hindi bilingual [biographical](/wiki/Biographical_film "Biographical film") [action film](/wiki/Action_film "Action film") *[Major](/wiki/Major_(film) "Major (film)")*, a biopic of [Sandeep Unnikrishnan](/wiki/Sandeep_Unnikrishnan "Sandeep Unnikrishnan") (played by [Adivi Sesh](/wiki/Adivi_Sesh "Adivi Sesh")), in which she played the role of Isha Agarwal who is Unnikrishnan's love interest.[[7]](#cite_note-7)[[8]](#cite_note-8) The film received critical acclaim and became one of the highest grossing Telugu films of 2022.[[9]](#cite_note-9)

Manjrekar was next appeared in *[Kuch Khattaa Ho Jaay](/wiki/Kuch_Khattaa_Ho_Jaay "Kuch Khattaa Ho Jaay")* opposite [Guru Randhawa](/wiki/Guru_Randhawa "Guru Randhawa").[[10]](#cite_note-10)
