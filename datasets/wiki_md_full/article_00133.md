National highway in India

**National Highway 215** (**NH 215**) is a [National Highway](/wiki/National_Highway_(India) "National Highway (India)") in India. It starts from Mahadevpur and terminates at Dibrugarh in the state of Assam.[[1]](#cite_note-renumber-1)
