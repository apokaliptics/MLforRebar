Montenegrin footballer

**Marko Vukasović** (born 10 September 1990) is a [Montenegrin](/wiki/Montenegrins_(ethnic_group) "Montenegrins (ethnic group)") [footballer](/wiki/Association_football "Association football") who plays for Hungarian club [Mosonmagyaróvár](/wiki/Mosonmagyar%C3%B3v%C3%A1ri_TE "Mosonmagyaróvári TE").

He is the older brother of [Mladen Vukasović](/wiki/Mladen_Vukasovi%C4%87 "Mladen Vukasović").[[1]](#cite_note-1)
