Place in Littoral, Slovenia

**Matavun** (pronounced [[mataˈʋuːn]](/wiki/Help:IPA/Slovene "Help:IPA/Slovene"); [Italian](/wiki/Italian_language "Italian language"): *Mattauno*) is a settlement near the entrance to [Škocjan Caves](/wiki/%C5%A0kocjan_Caves "Škocjan Caves") in the [Municipality of Divača](/wiki/Municipality_of_Diva%C4%8Da "Municipality of Divača") in the [Littoral](/wiki/Slovenian_Littoral "Slovenian Littoral") region of [Slovenia](/wiki/Slovenia "Slovenia").[[2]](#cite_note-2)
