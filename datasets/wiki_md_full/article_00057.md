Sports season



The **2016 Conference USA men's soccer season** was the 22nd season of men's varsity soccer in the conference.

The [FIU Panthers](/wiki/FIU_Panthers_men%27s_soccer "FIU Panthers men's soccer") are both the defending conference tournament champions. The [Kentucky Wildcats](/wiki/Kentucky_Wildcats_men%27s_soccer "Kentucky Wildcats men's soccer") are the defending regular season champions.
