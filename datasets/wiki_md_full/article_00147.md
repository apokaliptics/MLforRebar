Finnish rally driver



**Anton Alén** (born 3 June 1983) is a [Finnish](/wiki/Finland "Finland") [rally](/wiki/Rallying "Rallying") driver, who competed in [World Rally Championship](/wiki/World_Rally_Championship "World Rally Championship") and [Intercontinental Rally Challenge](/wiki/Intercontinental_Rally_Challenge "Intercontinental Rally Challenge") during his career. He was a factory driver for Fiat Abarth from 2007 to 2009. He scored one IRC victory, at [2007 Rally Russia](/w/index.php?title=2007_Rally_Russia&action=edit&redlink=1 "2007 Rally Russia (page does not exist)"). Hís father [Markku Alén](/wiki/Markku_Al%C3%A9n "Markku Alén")[[1]](#cite_note-1) became one-time [World Rally Champion](/wiki/World_Rally_Champion "World Rally Champion") in [1978](/wiki/1978_World_Rally_Championship_season "1978 World Rally Championship season") with the legendary [Fiat 131](/wiki/Fiat_131 "Fiat 131") [Abarth](/wiki/Abarth "Abarth").
