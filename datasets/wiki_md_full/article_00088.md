Italian cyclist (born 1991)

**Rossella Callovi** (born 5 April 1991) is an Italian professional road and track [racing cyclist](/wiki/Bicycle_racing "Bicycle racing").[[1]](#cite_note-ProCyclingStats-1) On the road she won as junior the gold medal in the road race at 2009 junior world championships in Moscow and the silver medal in the road race at the 2008 junior world championships in Cape Town. At the [2010 UCI Road World Championships](/wiki/2010_UCI_Road_World_Championships "2010 UCI Road World Championships") she competed in the women's Ã¨lite road race. On the track she won as a junior the bronze medal in the Junior Women's Team Pursuit at the [2008 UEC European Track Championships](/wiki/2008_UEC_European_Track_Championships "2008 UEC European Track Championships"). At the [2010 European Track Championships](/wiki/2010_European_Track_Championships "2010 European Track Championships") she competed in the women's team pursuit.
