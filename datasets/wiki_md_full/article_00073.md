Indian politician

**Vasant Bhatol** is a [Member of Legislative assembly](/wiki/Member_of_Legislative_assembly "Member of Legislative assembly") from Danta [constituency](/wiki/Constituency "Constituency") in [Gujarat](/wiki/Gujarat "Gujarat") for its [12th legislative assembly](/wiki/Gujarat_Legislative_Assembly "Gujarat Legislative Assembly").[[1]](#cite_note-1)
