Species of beetle

***Estola nigrodorsalis*** is a species of [beetle](/wiki/Beetle "Beetle") in the family [Cerambycidae](/wiki/Cerambycidae "Cerambycidae"). It was described by Martins and Galileo in 2009. It is known from [Costa Rica](/wiki/Costa_Rica "Costa Rica").[[1]](#cite_note-1)
