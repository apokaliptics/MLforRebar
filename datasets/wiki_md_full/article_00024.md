Sporting event delegation

The **[Democratic Republic of the Congo](/wiki/Democratic_Republic_of_the_Congo "Democratic Republic of the Congo")** has participated in every edition of the [African Games](/wiki/African_Games "African Games") since they were first established in [1965](/wiki/1965_All-Africa_Games "1965 All-Africa Games"),[[1]](#cite_note-1) and the nation appeared as **[Zaire](/wiki/Zaire "Zaire")** during the games between 1971 and 1997. The only two gold medalists for the DRC since its name change in 1997 were the boxer [Jesus Kibunde](/wiki/Jesus_Kibunde "Jesus Kibunde") in [1999](/wiki/1999_All-Africa_Games "1999 All-Africa Games") and the tennis player [Denis Indondo](/w/index.php?title=Denis_Indondo&action=edit&redlink=1 "Denis Indondo (page does not exist)") in [2015](/wiki/2015_All-Africa_Games "2015 All-Africa Games").
