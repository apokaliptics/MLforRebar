American politician

**Stanley Paige Zeigler Jr.** is an American politician who served as a member of the [Maine House of Representatives](/wiki/Maine_House_of_Representatives "Maine House of Representatives") from December 2016 to December 2024.

## Early career

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Stanley_Zeigler&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Early career")]

He has worked as a merchant marine deck officer, a special needs [teacher](/wiki/Teacher "Teacher"), a [lumberjack](/wiki/Lumberjack "Lumberjack"), work with a [drug rehabilitation](/wiki/Drug_rehabilitation "Drug rehabilitation") center, an [EMT](/wiki/Emergency_medical_technician "Emergency medical technician"), a [road crew](/wiki/Road_crew "Road crew") member, and a [darkroom](/wiki/Darkroom "Darkroom") technician.[[3]](#cite_note-3)

## Electoral history

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Stanley_Zeigler&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Electoral history")]

He was first elected to the 96th district in the [2016 Maine House of Representatives election](/w/index.php?title=2016_Maine_House_of_Representatives_election&action=edit&redlink=1 "2016 Maine House of Representatives election (page does not exist)"). He was reelected in the [2018 Maine House of Representatives election](/wiki/2018_Maine_House_of_Representatives_election "2018 Maine House of Representatives election") and the [2020 Maine House of Representatives election](/wiki/2020_Maine_House_of_Representatives_election "2020 Maine House of Representatives election"). He was redistricted to the 40th district and elected in the [2022 Maine House of Representatives election](/wiki/2022_Maine_House_of_Representatives_election "2022 Maine House of Representatives election").

## Biography

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Stanley_Zeigler&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Biography")]

Zeigler earned a [bachelor's degree](/wiki/Bachelor%27s_degree "Bachelor's degree") from [Dickinson College](/wiki/Dickinson_College "Dickinson College") in 1971 and an [associate degree](/wiki/Associate_degree "Associate degree") from [Southern Maine Community College](/wiki/Southern_Maine_Community_College "Southern Maine Community College").[[4]](#cite_note-4)
