French politician

**Amédée de Béjarry** (30 June 1840 - 1 October 1916) was a French politician. He served as a member of the [French Senate](/wiki/French_Senate "French Senate") from 1886 to 1916, representing [Vendée](/wiki/Vend%C3%A9e "Vendée").[[1]](#cite_note-senatebio-1)
