Species of beetle

***Saperda bilabilis*** is a species of [beetle](/wiki/Beetle "Beetle") in the family [Cerambycidae](/wiki/Cerambycidae "Cerambycidae"). It was described by Newman in 1850.[[1]](#cite_note-1)
