Species of moth

***Rachiplusia nu*** is a species of [moth](/wiki/Moth "Moth") in the family [Noctuidae](/wiki/Noctuidae "Noctuidae"). It is found in South America, including Paraguay, Uruguay, southern Brazil, Argentina and Chile.

The [wingspan](/wiki/Wingspan "Wingspan") is 28–34 mm.

Larvae have been recorded on tobacco, sunflower, soybean, alfalfa, clover, tomato, spinach and number of other plants.[*[citation needed](/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed "Wikipedia:Citation needed")*]
