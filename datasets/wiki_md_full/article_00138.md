**Logothetidis** or **Logothetides** ([Greek](/wiki/Greek_language "Greek language"): Λογοθετίδης) is a Greek surname. The feminine form is **Logothetidou** (Λογοθετίδου). Notable people with the surname include:

* [Leon Sculy Logothetides](/wiki/Leon_Sculy_Logothetides "Leon Sculy Logothetides") (1853–1912), Romanian surgeon and politician
* [Stergios Logothetidis](/wiki/Stergios_Logothetidis "Stergios Logothetidis") (born 1953), Greek physicist
* [Vasilis Logothetidis](/wiki/Vasilis_Logothetidis "Vasilis Logothetidis") (1897–1960), Greek comedian
