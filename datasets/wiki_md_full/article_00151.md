Football league season

The **2018–19 Nemzeti Bajnokság II** (also known as 2018–19 Merkantil Bank Liga) was Hungary's 68th season of the Nemzeti Bajnokság II, the second tier of the [Hungarian football league system](/wiki/Hungarian_football_league_system "Hungarian football league system").
