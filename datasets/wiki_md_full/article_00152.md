Rugby team

The **Berkeley All Blues** are a women's [rugby union](/wiki/Rugby_union "Rugby union") club based in [Berkeley, California](/wiki/Berkeley,_California "Berkeley, California"). They were founded in 1978 and are founding members of the [Women's Premier League Rugby](/wiki/Women%27s_Premier_League_Rugby "Women's Premier League Rugby"). They field three teams throughout the year, in the WPL, Division II, and in [Sevens](/wiki/Rugby_sevens "Rugby sevens"). The All Blues have won a total of 16 National Championships, they include the 2011 and 2012 Women's Premier League Championships and the 2011 and 2013 Women's [Club 7s National Championships](/wiki/USA_Rugby_Club_7s "USA Rugby Club 7s").[[1]](#cite_note-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2)
