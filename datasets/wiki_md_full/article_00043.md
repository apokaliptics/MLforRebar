2015 single by St. Vincent

"**Teenage Talk**" is a song written and performed by American [art rock](/wiki/Art_rock "Art rock") musician [St. Vincent](/wiki/St._Vincent_(musician) "St. Vincent (musician)"), released [digitally](/wiki/Music_download "Music download") on April 7, 2015.[[1]](#cite_note-itunes-1)

## Promotion and release

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Teenage_Talk&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Promotion and release")]

St. Vincent initially composed the song for inclusion on her [eponymous album](/wiki/St._Vincent_(album) "St. Vincent (album)") released in 2014, but the song did not make the album's final track listing.[[2]](#cite_note-slate-2) A snippet of the song was used prior to its official release during the [fourth season](/wiki/List_of_Girls_episodes#Season_4_(2015) "List of Girls episodes") of the [HBO](/wiki/HBO "HBO") television series *[Girls](/wiki/Girls_(TV_series) "Girls (TV series)")*, in the episode "Tad & Loreen & Avi & Shanaz".[[3]](#cite_note-makers-3)[[4]](#cite_note-genius-4) St. Vincent performed the song live for the first time on April 6, 2015 on *[The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon](/wiki/The_Tonight_Show_Starring_Jimmy_Fallon "The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon")*;[[4]](#cite_note-genius-4)[[5]](#cite_note-pitchfork-5) and the song was officially released via [iTunes](/wiki/ITunes "ITunes") on April 7.[[1]](#cite_note-itunes-1)

## Critical reception

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Teenage_Talk&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Critical reception")]

The song has received positive reviews. Sharan Shetty of *[Slate](/wiki/Slate_(magazine) "Slate (magazine)")* called the song "wistful" and "a pensive coming-of-age tune".[[2]](#cite_note-slate-2) Chris DeVille of *[Stereogum](/wiki/Stereogum "Stereogum")* also praised the song, further stating that it is "more moody and straightforward" than St. Vincent's previous work.[[6]](#cite_note-stereogum-6)
