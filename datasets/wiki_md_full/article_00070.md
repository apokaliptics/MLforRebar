Croatian basketball player and coach

**Vladimir Krstić** (born 27 July 1972) is a Croatian professional [basketball](/wiki/Basketball "Basketball") coach and former player who is the head coach for [Vëllaznimi](/wiki/KB_V%C3%ABllaznimi "KB Vëllaznimi") of the [Kosovo Superleague](/wiki/Kosovo_Basketball_Superleague "Kosovo Basketball Superleague").

## Professional career

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Vladimir_Krsti%C4%87_(basketball)&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Professional career")]

Krstić, an Osijek native, started his career in his hometown club [Olimpija Osijek](/wiki/KK_Olimpija_Osijek "KK Olimpija Osijek"). After leaving Osijek, Krstić played for series of clubs all around Europe - [Steiner Bayreuth](/wiki/Medi_Bayreuth "Medi Bayreuth"), [Cibona Zagreb](/wiki/Cibona_Zagreb "Cibona Zagreb"), [Anwil Wloclawek](/wiki/Anwil_Wloclawek "Anwil Wloclawek"), [Pau Orthez](/wiki/Pau_Orthez "Pau Orthez"), [Metis Varese](/wiki/Metis_Varese "Metis Varese"), [Lietuvos rytas](/wiki/Lietuvos_rytas "Lietuvos rytas"), [KK Zadar](/wiki/KK_Zadar "KK Zadar"), [Lianera Menorca](/wiki/Menorca_B%C3%A0squet "Menorca Bàsquet"), [Olimpia Larissa](/wiki/Olimpia_Larissa "Olimpia Larissa"), [BC Kyiv](/wiki/BC_Kyiv "BC Kyiv"), [Aigaleo](/wiki/Aigaleo_B.C. "Aigaleo B.C."), [KK Zagreb](/wiki/KK_Zagreb "KK Zagreb") and [Cedevita Zagreb](/wiki/Cedevita_Zagreb "Cedevita Zagreb").

## Coaching career

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Vladimir_Krsti%C4%87_(basketball)&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Coaching career")]

In December 2014, Krstić took over as a head coach of Croatian first division club [KK Vrijednosnice Osijek](/wiki/KK_Vrijednosnice_Osijek "KK Vrijednosnice Osijek"). On 9 January 2019, Krstić and Vrijednosnice parted ways.

In September 2017, he became an assistant coach of [Croatia national team](/wiki/Croatia_national_basketball_team "Croatia national basketball team") in staff of [Ivica Skelin](/wiki/Ivica_Skelin "Ivica Skelin"). He left the staff in 2019.

On 19 November 2019, Krstić was named the new head coach for [Dubrava Furnir](/wiki/KK_Dubrava "KK Dubrava") of the [Croatian League](/wiki/HT_Premijer_liga "HT Premijer liga").[[1]](#cite_note-1)

In July 2022, [Gorica](/wiki/KK_Gorica "KK Gorica") hired Krstić as their new head coach.[[2]](#cite_note-2)
