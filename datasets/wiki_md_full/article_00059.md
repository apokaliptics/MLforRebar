American politician



**Michael S. Sanchez** (born August 3, 1950)[[1]](#cite_note-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2) is an American attorney and politician who served in the [New Mexico Senate](/wiki/New_Mexico_Senate "New Mexico Senate") from 1993 to 2016, representing the 29th District ([Valencia](/wiki/Valencia_County,_New_Mexico "Valencia County, New Mexico") and [Bernalillo](/wiki/Bernalillo_County,_New_Mexico "Bernalillo County, New Mexico") Counties). He was defeated in his bid for re-election in 2016.

## Early life and education

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Michael_S._Sanchez&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Early life and education")]

Sanchez was born in [Belen, New Mexico](/wiki/Belen,_New_Mexico "Belen, New Mexico") to Gil and Priscilla Sanchez. He is the youngest of four children, including [Raymond G. Sanchez](/wiki/Raymond_G._Sanchez "Raymond G. Sanchez"), an attorney who served in the [New Mexico House of Representatives](/wiki/New_Mexico_House_of_Representatives "New Mexico House of Representatives"). Sanchez attended [Belen High School](/wiki/Belen_High_School "Belen High School") and moved to [Clarinda, Iowa](/wiki/Clarinda,_Iowa "Clarinda, Iowa") to attend [Iowa Western Community College](/wiki/Iowa_Western_Community_College "Iowa Western Community College"), where he played baseball and basketball. Sanchez finished school at [University of New Mexico](/wiki/University_of_New_Mexico "University of New Mexico"). Sanchez then earned a [Juris Doctor](/wiki/Juris_Doctor "Juris Doctor") from the [University of New Mexico School of Law](/wiki/University_of_New_Mexico_School_of_Law "University of New Mexico School of Law").

## Career

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Michael_S._Sanchez&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Career")]

After graduating from law school, Sanchez was admitted to the [State Bar of New Mexico](/wiki/State_Bar_of_New_Mexico "State Bar of New Mexico").

Before serving as a [State Senator](/wiki/State_Senator "State Senator"), Sanchez served as the county chairman of the Democratic Party in [Valencia County](/wiki/Valencia_County,_New_Mexico "Valencia County, New Mexico"). Sanchez was first elected to the State Senate in 1992.

During his years of service, Sanchez focused on creating better jobs, education and protecting working families. One of his notable legislative accomplishments was the creation of the New Mexico Lottery Scholarship, which has provided thousands of New Mexico high school students with financial assistance to attend college. The Lottery Scholarship was enacted in 1996.

Sanchez served as the Majority Leader of the [New Mexico State Senate](/wiki/New_Mexico_State_Senate "New Mexico State Senate").

## Personal life

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Michael_S._Sanchez&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Personal life")]

Sanchez married Lynn (Trembly) Sanchez in 1969. They have two sons and a daughter.
