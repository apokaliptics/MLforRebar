Village in Latvia

**Lielbērze** is a village in the [Auri Parish](/wiki/Auri_Parish "Auri Parish") of [Dobele Municipality](/wiki/Dobele_Municipality "Dobele Municipality") in the [Semigallia](/wiki/Semigallia "Semigallia") region and the [Zemgale Planning Region](/wiki/Zemgale_Planning_Region "Zemgale Planning Region") in [Latvia](/wiki/Latvia "Latvia").
