Slovenian badminton player (born 1988)

**Iztok Utroša** (born 1 March 1988) is a Slovenian [badminton](/wiki/Badminton "Badminton") player.[[1]](#cite_note-1) He competed at the [2015 European Games](/wiki/Badminton_at_the_2015_European_Games "Badminton at the 2015 European Games")[[2]](#cite_note-2)
