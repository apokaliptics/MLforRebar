Spanish writer and poet

**Juan Cánovas Ortega** is a [Spanish](/wiki/Spanish_people "Spanish people") short story writer and poet. He was born in 1961 in [Tarrasa](/wiki/Tarrasa "Tarrasa") and studied Modern History at the [Universidad Autónoma de Barcelona](/wiki/Universidad_Aut%C3%B3noma_de_Barcelona "Universidad Autónoma de Barcelona"), where he later worked as an administrator. He has won a number of literary prizes.[[1]](#cite_note-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2)
