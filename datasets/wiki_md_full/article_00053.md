Species of moth

***Calamotropha afra*** is a [moth](/wiki/Moth "Moth") in the family [Crambidae](/wiki/Crambidae "Crambidae"). It was described by Graziano Bassi in 1986.[[1]](#cite_note-1) It is found in [Zambia](/wiki/Zambia "Zambia").[[2]](#cite_note-2)
