2013 tennis event results

[Eugenie Bouchard](/wiki/Eugenie_Bouchard "Eugenie Bouchard") and [Jessica Pegula](/wiki/Jessica_Pegula "Jessica Pegula") were the defending champions, having won the event in 2012, but Bouchard chose not to participate in 2013. Pegula partnered up with [Emily Harman](/wiki/Emily_Harman "Emily Harman"), but lost in the quarterfinals to [Irina Falconi](/wiki/Irina_Falconi "Irina Falconi") and [Maria Sanchez](/wiki/Maria_Sanchez_(tennis) "Maria Sanchez (tennis)").

[Julia Cohen](/wiki/Julia_Cohen "Julia Cohen") and [Tatjana Maria](/wiki/Tatjana_Maria "Tatjana Maria") won the title, defeating Falconi and Sanchez in the final, 6–4, 4–6, [11–9].
