Municipality in Košice Region, Slovakia

**Hlivištia** ([Hungarian](/wiki/Hungarian_language "Hungarian language"): *Hegygombás*, [Rusyn](/wiki/Rusyn_language "Rusyn language"): Гливища) is a village and municipality in the [Sobrance District](/wiki/Sobrance_District "Sobrance District") in the [Košice Region](/wiki/Ko%C5%A1ice_Region "Košice Region") of east [Slovakia](/wiki/Slovakia "Slovakia").

## History

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Hlivi%C5%A1tia&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: History")]

In historical records the village was first mentioned in 1413. Before the establishment of independent [Czechoslovakia](/wiki/Czechoslovakia "Czechoslovakia") in 1918, Hlivištia was part of [Ung County](/wiki/Ung_County "Ung County") within the [Kingdom of Hungary](/wiki/Kingdom_of_Hungary "Kingdom of Hungary"). In 1939, it was for a short time part of the [Slovak Republic](/wiki/Slovak_Republic_(1939%E2%80%931945) "Slovak Republic (1939–1945)"). As a result of the [Slovak–Hungarian War](/wiki/Slovak%E2%80%93Hungarian_War "Slovak–Hungarian War") of 1939, it was again part of [Hungary](/wiki/Hungary "Hungary") from 1939 to 1944. On 2 November 1944, the [Red Army](/wiki/Red_Army "Red Army") entered Hlivištia and it was once again part of Czechoslovakia.
