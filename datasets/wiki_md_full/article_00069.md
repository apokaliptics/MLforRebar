Species of frog

***Dendropsophus giesleri*** is a species of [frog](/wiki/Frog "Frog") in the family [Hylidae](/wiki/Hylidae "Hylidae").
It is [endemic](/wiki/Endemism "Endemism") to [Brazil](/wiki/Brazil "Brazil").
Its natural [habitats](/wiki/Habitat "Habitat") are subtropical or tropical moist lowland [forests](/wiki/Forest "Forest"), subtropical or tropical moist [montane forests](/wiki/Montane_forest "Montane forest"), freshwater [marshes](/wiki/Marsh "Marsh"), and intermittent freshwater marshes.
It is threatened by [habitat loss](/wiki/Habitat_loss "Habitat loss").
