Brazilian singer-songwriter (born 1997)



**Caroline dos Reis Biazin** (born 22 April 1997) is a Brazilian singer-songwriter and actress.

## Early life

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Carol_Biazin&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Early life")]

Born in [Campo Mourão](/wiki/Campo_Mour%C3%A3o "Campo Mourão"), Biazin grew up between Campo Mourão, [São João do Ivaí](/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Jo%C3%A3o_do_Iva%C3%AD "São João do Ivaí") and [Curitiba](/wiki/Curitiba "Curitiba").[[1]](#cite_note-G1-1) The daughter of two shopkeepers, she has two older siblings.[[2]](#cite_note-gl-2) At young age she got guitar lessons, and her first guitar was a [Giannini](/wiki/Giannini_(guitar_company) "Giannini (guitar company)").[[1]](#cite_note-G1-1) She studied Popular Music at the [University of the State of Paraná](/wiki/University_of_the_State_of_Paran%C3%A1 "University of the State of Paraná").[[2]](#cite_note-gl-2)

## Career

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=Carol_Biazin&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Career")]

After moving to [São Paulo](/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Paulo "São Paulo") to pursue a singing career, in 2016 Biazin competed in the first edition of *[X-Factor](/wiki/X_Factor_(Brazilian_TV_series) "X Factor (Brazilian TV series)")*, and in 2017 she was runner-up in *[The Voice Brasil](/wiki/The_Voice_Brasil "The Voice Brasil")*.[[1]](#cite_note-G1-1)[[3]](#cite_note-bio-3)

In 2019, Biazin made her record debut with the EP *S*, which got her the [Prêmio Jovem Brasileiro](/wiki/Pr%C3%AAmio_Jovem_Brasileiro "Prêmio Jovem Brasileiro") as Artist of the Year.[[3]](#cite_note-bio-3) In 2021, she released her first album *Beijo de Judas*.[[3]](#cite_note-bio-3) In 2022, she received the [MTV Miaw Award](/wiki/MTV_MIAW_Awards "MTV MIAW Awards") in the "Orgulho do Vale" (Pride of the Valley) category.[[3]](#cite_note-bio-3) In 2023, she released her second album *Reversa*.[[1]](#cite_note-G1-1)[[3]](#cite_note-bio-3) The same year, she was nominated for [Multishow Brazilian Music Award](/wiki/Multishow_Brazilian_Music_Award "Multishow Brazilian Music Award") as revelation of the year.[[4]](#cite_note-4)

Biazin is also active as a songwriter for other artists, including [Luísa Sonza](/wiki/Lu%C3%ADsa_Sonza "Luísa Sonza"), [Anitta](/wiki/Anitta_(singer) "Anitta (singer)"), [Rouge](/wiki/Rouge_(group) "Rouge (group)"), [Claudia Leitte](/wiki/Claudia_Leitte "Claudia Leitte"), [Juliette](/wiki/Juliette_(Brazilian_singer) "Juliette (Brazilian singer)") and [Vitão](/w/index.php?title=Vit%C3%A3o_(singer)&action=edit&redlink=1 "Vitão (singer) (page does not exist)") [[pt](https://pt.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vit%C3%A3o "pt:Vitão")].[[1]](#cite_note-G1-1)[[2]](#cite_note-gl-2)[[5]](#cite_note-5)
