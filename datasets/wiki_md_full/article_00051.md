The **Lago-Naki plateau** ([Russian](/wiki/Russian_language "Russian language"): Лаго-Наки) is a plateau in the [Western Caucasus](/wiki/Western_Caucasus "Western Caucasus") at an altitude of up to 2,200 meters known for its [alpine meadows](/wiki/Alpine_meadows "Alpine meadows"). Nearly all of it is within the [Maykopsky District](/wiki/Maykopsky_District "Maykopsky District"), [Adygea](/wiki/Adygea "Adygea"), Russia.[[1]](#cite_note-1)
