1989 single by Big Audio Dynamite

**"James Brown"** is a 1989 single by the English band [Big Audio Dynamite](/wiki/Big_Audio_Dynamite "Big Audio Dynamite") from their fourth studio album *[Megatop Phoenix](/wiki/Megatop_Phoenix "Megatop Phoenix")* (1989) that peaked at No. 2 on the US *[Billboard](/wiki/Billboard_(magazine) "Billboard (magazine)")* [Modern Rock Tracks](/wiki/Alternative_Airplay "Alternative Airplay") chart.

## Track listing

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=James_Brown_(song)&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Track listing")]

All tracks written by [Mick Jones](/wiki/Mick_Jones_(The_Clash_guitarist) "Mick Jones (The Clash guitarist)") and [Don Letts](/wiki/Don_Letts "Don Letts"); except "If I Were John Carpenter" written by BAD.

**12" vinyl**

1. "James Brown (Remix)" – 6:51
2. "James Brown (Remix Edit)" – 3:57
3. "If I Were John Carpenter" – 7:24
4. "James Brown (LP Version)" – 5:11

## Chart performance

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=James_Brown_(song)&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Chart performance")]

* "James Brown" and "If I Were John Carpenter" charted together on the *[Billboard](/wiki/Billboard_(magazine) "Billboard (magazine)")* [Hot Dance Club Play](/wiki/Dance_Club_Songs "Dance Club Songs") chart.
