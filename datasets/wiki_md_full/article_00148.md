1971 film directed by Rafael Romero Marchent

***Sartana Kills Them All*** ([Spanish](/wiki/Spanish_language "Spanish language"): ***Un par de asesinos***) is a 1970 Spanish [crime comedy](/wiki/Crime_comedy "Crime comedy") [western film](/wiki/Western_film "Western film") directed by [Rafael Romero Marchent](/wiki/Rafael_Romero_Marchent "Rafael Romero Marchent"), written by Mario Alabiso and Santiago Moncada and starring [Gianni Garko](/wiki/Gianni_Garko "Gianni Garko") (reprising his role from the official [*Sartana* film series](/wiki/Sartana "Sartana")), Guglielmo Spoletini and [María Silva](/wiki/Mar%C3%ADa_Silva_(actress) "María Silva (actress)").[[1]](#cite_note-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2)
