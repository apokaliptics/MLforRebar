**Oskari Mantere's cabinet** was the 16th government of [Republic of Finland](/wiki/Republic_of_Finland "Republic of Finland"). Cabinet's time period was from December 22, 1928 to August 16, 1929.[[1]](#cite_note-1) It was a [minority government](/wiki/Minority_government "Minority government").
