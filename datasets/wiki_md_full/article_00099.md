1980 single by Larry Gatlin & the Gatlin Brothers

"**Taking Somebody with Me When I Fall**" is a song written by Larry Gatlin, and recorded by American [country music](/wiki/Country_music "Country music") group [Larry Gatlin & the Gatlin Brothers](/wiki/Larry_Gatlin_%26_the_Gatlin_Brothers "Larry Gatlin & the Gatlin Brothers"). It was released in March 1980 as the third single from the album *Straight Ahead*. The song reached number 12 on the *[Billboard](/wiki/Billboard_(magazine) "Billboard (magazine)")* [Hot Country Singles & Tracks](/wiki/Hot_Country_Songs "Hot Country Songs") chart.[[1]](#cite_note-1)
