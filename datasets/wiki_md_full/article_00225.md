The **2010 CONSUR Women's Sevens** was the tournaments sixth edition and was held from 5 to 7 January in [Mar del Plata](/wiki/Mar_del_Plata "Mar del Plata"), [Argentina](/wiki/Argentina "Argentina").[[1]](#cite_note-:0-1)[[2]](#cite_note-:1-2) [Brazil](/wiki/Brazil_women%27s_national_rugby_sevens_team "Brazil women's national rugby sevens team") won their sixth consecutive title after successfully defending it against [Colombia](/wiki/Colombia_women%27s_national_rugby_sevens_team "Colombia women's national rugby sevens team") in the Cup final.[[3]](#cite_note-3)[[4]](#cite_note-4)

## Teams

[[edit](/w/index.php?title=2010_CONSUR_Women%27s_Sevens&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Teams")]

Eight teams competed at the tournament.

* [Argentina](/wiki/Argentina_women%27s_national_rugby_sevens_team "Argentina women's national rugby sevens team")
* [Brazil](/wiki/Brazil_women%27s_national_rugby_sevens_team "Brazil women's national rugby sevens team")
* [Chile](/wiki/Chile_women%27s_national_rugby_sevens_team "Chile women's national rugby sevens team")
* [Colombia](/wiki/Colombia_women%27s_national_rugby_sevens_team "Colombia women's national rugby sevens team")
* [Paraguay](/w/index.php?title=Paraguay_women%27s_national_rugby_sevens_team&action=edit&redlink=1 "Paraguay women's national rugby sevens team (page does not exist)")
* [Peru](/w/index.php?title=Peru_women%27s_national_rugby_sevens_team&action=edit&redlink=1 "Peru women's national rugby sevens team (page does not exist)")
* [Uruguay](/w/index.php?title=Uruguay_women%27s_national_rugby_sevens_team&action=edit&redlink=1 "Uruguay women's national rugby sevens team (page does not exist)")
* [Venezuela](/wiki/Venezuela_women%27s_national_rugby_sevens_team "Venezuela women's national rugby sevens team")
