Belarusian cyclist (born 1998)

**Argiro Milaki** (born 3 July 1992) is a Greek [racing cyclist](/wiki/Bicycle_racing "Bicycle racing"), who currently rides for [UCI Women's Continental Team](/wiki/UCI_Women%27s_Team "UCI Women's Team") [Aromitalia 3T Vaiano](/wiki/Aromitalia_3T_Vaiano "Aromitalia 3T Vaiano").

She is a seven time [Greek national road race champion](/wiki/Greek_National_Road_Race_Championships "Greek National Road Race Championships") and two time [time trial champion](/wiki/Greek_National_Time_Trial_Championships "Greek National Time Trial Championships").[[3]](#cite_note-3) She also competes in [track cycling](/wiki/Track_cycling "Track cycling"), where she has won a number of national titles in various events.

In 2012 and 2016, Milaki competed as a tandem pilot at the [Paralympic Games](/wiki/Paralympic_Games "Paralympic Games"), leading the visually impaired rider [Adamantia Chalkiadaki](/w/index.php?title=Adamantia_Chalkiadaki&action=edit&redlink=1 "Adamantia Chalkiadaki (page does not exist)").[[4]](#cite_note-bicitv-Team_Ser-4) At the [2017 UCI Para-cycling Road World Championships](/wiki/2017_UCI_Para-cycling_Road_World_Championships "2017 UCI Para-cycling Road World Championships"), she finished third in the road race leading [Eleni Kalatzi](/w/index.php?title=Eleni_Kalatzi&action=edit&redlink=1 "Eleni Kalatzi (page does not exist)").
