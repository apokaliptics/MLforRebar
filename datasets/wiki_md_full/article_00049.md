Village in Lublin Voivodeship, Poland

**Bronisławka** [[brɔniˈswafka]](/wiki/Help:IPA/Polish "Help:IPA/Polish") is a [village](/wiki/Village "Village") in the administrative district of [Gmina Grabowiec](/wiki/Gmina_Grabowiec "Gmina Grabowiec"), within [Zamość County](/wiki/Zamo%C5%9B%C4%87_County "Zamość County"), [Lublin Voivodeship](/wiki/Lublin_Voivodeship "Lublin Voivodeship"), in eastern Poland.[[1]](#cite_note-TERYT-1)
