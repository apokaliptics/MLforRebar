French sprinter

**Jean-Paul Martin-du-Gard** (3 May 1927 – 26 February 2017) was a French runner who competed in the [1952 Summer Olympics](/wiki/1952_Summer_Olympics "1952 Summer Olympics") and in the [1956 Summer Olympics](/wiki/1956_Summer_Olympics "1956 Summer Olympics").[[1]](#cite_note-sref-1)[[2]](#cite_note-2)
