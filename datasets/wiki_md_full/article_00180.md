Ghost town in Colorado, United States

**Sopris** was an [unincorporated community](/wiki/Unincorporated_area "Unincorporated area") located in [Las Animas County](/wiki/Las_Animas_County,_Colorado "Las Animas County, Colorado"), [Colorado](/wiki/Colorado "Colorado"), United States. The town is now under the surface of [Trinidad Lake](/wiki/Trinidad_Lake "Trinidad Lake") in [Trinidad Lake State Park](/wiki/Trinidad_Lake_State_Park "Trinidad Lake State Park").[[1]](#cite_note-GR3-1)

The [U.S. Post Office](/wiki/United_States_Postal_Service "United States Postal Service") at [Trinidad](/wiki/Trinidad,_Colorado "Trinidad, Colorado") ([ZIP Code](/wiki/ZIP_Code "ZIP Code") 81082) now serves Sopris postal addresses.[[2]](#cite_note-ZIPcode-2)

The community was named after General E. B. Sopris, a local land owner.[[3]](#cite_note-3)
